## Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Project is designed to promote awareness and understanding of essential digital skills required in today’s technology-driven world. The project focuses on helping individuals become more confident and responsible digital users by covering important topics such as internet safety, online communication, digital tools, cybersecurity awareness, and responsible use of technology.

The main objective of this project is to highlight the importance of digital literacy in everyday life, education, and future careers. It aims to make users more informed about how to access, evaluate, create, and share information online safely and effectively.

Through this project, learners can gain practical knowledge about navigating the digital world, protecting their personal information, and using online platforms productively. Overall, this project serves as a simple yet meaningful step toward building a more digitally aware and empowered society.
