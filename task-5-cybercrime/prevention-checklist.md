# Stay Safe Online – Phishing Prevention Checklist

* Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or messages.
* Always check the sender’s email address carefully before trusting it.
* Avoid entering personal information on unknown or unverified websites.
* Look for secure websites (https://) before entering login details.
* Do not download attachments from unknown or suspicious sources.
* Be cautious of messages creating urgency or fear, such as “your account will be blocked.”
* Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
* Regularly update your passwords and account security settings.
* Report phishing attempts at **National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in** or call **Helpline 1930**.
