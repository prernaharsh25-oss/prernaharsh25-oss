# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

## Do’s

* Maintain a professional profile and username
* Think before posting any content online
* Respect others and communicate politely
* Verify information before sharing
* Use privacy settings to protect personal data

## Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling
* Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news
* Do not use unprofessional language in public posts
